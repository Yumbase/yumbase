Entrepreneurship

We’re here for anyone who wants to master real digital skills.

FEBRUARY 1st, 2026   |   BEKARYS BERDESH

July 2025, I launched this server with a simple but ambitious goal: to create a community for people passionate about building startups and mastering real digital skills. I wanted a space where aspiring founders, creators, and digital learners could connect, share ideas, and grow together—without the noise and distractions found in larger communities.

From the very beginning, I committed myself fully to this mission. Some days, I would spend 3–4 hours helping members brainstorm ideas, solve technical problems, or navigate the challenges of starting and growing a business. Every discussion, resource, and piece of advice shared was guided by one principle: to help members take real steps forward in their projects and careers.

Building a community is never easy, and there were moments of trial and error. But each challenge was an opportunity to refine the space, making it more focused, supportive, and practical. Over time, we developed a culture where members feel comfortable asking questions, experimenting with ideas, and celebrating successes—big or small.

What makes this community unique is the focus on actionable growth. We’re not just talking about theory; we’re about turning ideas into tangible results. Whether it’s building a SaaS app, learning to leverage AI tools, or growing a small business, our members actively support each other to take the next step.

Today, I’m proud of the community we’ve built. It’s more than just a server—it’s a launchpad for creators and entrepreneurs who are serious about developing skills and scaling their ideas. And while we’ve come a long way, we continue to evolve, always striving to provide an even more supportive, resource-rich environment for anyone ready to take control of their growth and future.
